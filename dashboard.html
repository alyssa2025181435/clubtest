About Curriculum Club

The Curriculum Club is a student-driven learning hub that teaches practical skills, academic support, and personal growth. Members participate in workshops on web design, coding, and creative projects, while also developing leadership, teamwork, and communication skills through committee roles and events. Our mission is to empower learners with knowledge, creativity, and collaboration.
