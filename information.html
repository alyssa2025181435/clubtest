Membership Plan Information

Understand exactly what you get in each plan: the types of events, what the newsletter includes, how project submissions work, voting, workshops, and support.

Basic

RM20 / month

For members who want to attend events and stay connected without project responsibilities.

  • Access to events: Community meetups, open talks, showcase nights, and social gatherings.
  • Club newsletter: Monthly updates with upcoming events, learning tips, member highlights, and calls to join.
  • Project submissions: Not included — Basic members attend but don’t submit projects.
  • Voting rights: Not included.

Standard

RM50 / month

For active members who want to submit projects, vote on club decisions, and help shape the club.

  • Access to events: All Basic events plus hands-on workshops, peer review sessions, and contributor meetings.
  • Club newsletter: Includes early access to workshops, contributor columns, and resource packs.
  • Project submissions: Members can submit websites, designs, research, or event proposals. Reviewed by committee and showcased if approved.
  • Voting rights: Vote on event priorities, budget allocations, and club policies.

Premium

RM100 / month

For dedicated members who want exclusive workshops, priority support, and full participation privileges.

  • Access to events: All Standard events plus masterclasses, mentor sessions, and priority seating.
  • Club newsletter: Premium column with advanced guides, early announcements, and spotlight coverage.
  • Project submissions: Priority review, advanced submissions, and optional mentor pairing for complex projects.
  • Voting rights: Extended influence on strategic themes, flagship projects, and sponsorships.
  • Exclusive workshops: Advanced topics, limited group sizes, and project clinics.
  • Priority support: Faster responses, office hours with mentors, and resource assistance.
Tip: You can upgrade or downgrade between plans monthly. For plan changes, contact the committee via the Members page.
